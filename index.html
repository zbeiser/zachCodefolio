About Me

From herbs to HTML - from soil to CSS - from worms to webpages. This organic gardener is organizing his github. I've always been passionate about connecting with people through food, nature, and recreation. Now I use web design to make that connection global, through projects like creating resources for growing food or connecting people to their local bike trails. The web is a tool, its most powerful function being connection. My goal is to use that connection to change lives for the better.
